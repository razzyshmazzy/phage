By Nico Zametto

As I write this, I am entering my Sophomore year at Marin Catholic High School. I am Co-President of the Computer Science Club alongside another Sophomore, and I like to read. A lot. Like a lot a lot. You name it (and as long as it’s not romance) I’ve probably read it.

On this blog, I’ll speak about personal experiences with tech, Arduino, programming, AI, etc. And really just any ideas that come up during my daily life that I want to explore, develop, and share.

There will always be a philosophical framework in play on top of everything discussed here. So if you’re into that, perfect! If not, no worries! There’ll be a wealth of science experiments and electronics projects discussed here too—and who knows? You just might find that sprinkle of inspiration you’ve been looking for, lingering within Phage, waiting for you! :)

Enjoy Phage.
